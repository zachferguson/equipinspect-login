Inspired by Inspirobot, jsvine, and NeverForgetY2K A Markov Chain is a sentence (in this case) created by taking a book, and creating a weighted analysis of words and their neighbors, to generate new sentences that sound similar to ones previously created by the original author. This whimsical use aside, Markov Chains are one way computers are getting better at talking to us.
